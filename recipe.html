Recipe: Veg Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Boiled potato
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Apply butter on one side of the bread slices.
  2. Add sliced potatoes, tomato, and onion.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  4. Cover with another bread slice.
  5. Toast or grill the sandwich.
  6. Serve hot with sauce.

Enjoy your homemade Veg Sandwich 😊
